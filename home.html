About RED
↓

Responsible web design that aims to educate and spread awareness of environmental issues.

We are passionate about nature, the environment and everything about it. Here at RED, we believe in the power of education. We believe that it is not only our generation's responsibilty to try and correct the damage that we have already done to the world, but also to educate future generations on the dangers of our current lifestyles and how they can change their lives to help the world.

Instead of using pushy techniques to scare and guilt people into "caring" for the environment, we prefer to nuture peoples' love and passion for nature, and educate them on how they can help in simple, or drastic ways.

We design websites, games and apps that follow educational, fun and enticing themes that above all spread environmental awareness.

We also aim to make every project paper free and as environmentally friendly as possible. Where we can, we try to guide our partners about environmental issues and how they can help to support them.
